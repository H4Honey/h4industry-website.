Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of honey do you sell?

We sell premium quality Sarso, Sfeda, and Kikar honey — all 100% pure and naturally extracted.

2. Is your honey raw and unprocessed?

Yes! Our honey is raw, unheated, and unprocessed to preserve all its natural enzymes and nutrients.

3. How should I store the honey?

Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. No need to refrigerate.

4. Does the honey expire?

Pure honey doesn’t expire if stored properly. However, we recommend using it within 18 months for best quality.

5. What is the crystallization in honey?

Crystallization is natural and a sign of purity. Place the jar in warm water to liquify it again if you prefer.
